Our COVID-19 response for corporate catering solutions

When it comes to delivering food to your office, we understand the necessity of a solution with complete transparency, responsibility and peace of mind, that's why we only delivery our meals with our team members and never allow 3rd party delivery service. A solution built for the new normal.

Safety is our number one priority

Beyond individually packaged meals, and the fact the our deliveries are only made by our internal delivery team. Our comprehensive Covid-19 plan ensures accountability and traceability and a response plan covering our clients, restaurants and all hungerhub staff. Our policy plan is updated regularly and posted on this page for your review.

Our comprehensive Covid-19 plan for office food delivery

As COVID-19 continues to evolve in Canada and around the world, we are ensuring that the health and safety of all clients and partners is our number one priority. We have introduced new policies and made precautionary changes to our network, in addition to following the recommendations and guidelines set out by the World Health Organization and the Government of Canada.
